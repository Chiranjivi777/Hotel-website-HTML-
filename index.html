Dosa Triva

Dosa/Dosai is being made for the past 2000 years and has originated from South India!

The original Tamil Dosa was softer and thicker.The thinner and crispier version of Dosa, which is popular all over India, was first made in Karnataka. The Dosa is considered to be healthy. It contains very little fat content and is rich in carbohydrates, and contains no sugar.Dosa varieties have constantly been experimented with and at last count there have been 624 varieties of Dosas!(Source:Dosa facts)
